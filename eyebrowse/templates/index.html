share and compare your web trails

eyebrowse is an add-on for firefox that lets you easily record, visualize, and share your trails through the web in real-time. Tell eyebrowse what sites to track, browse away and compare!

Find out what's hot, who's reading what, and how you surfing changes over time. Plus, contribute your web trails to science!

We give you control You tell eyebrowse explicitly what you want to share on a per-site basis. You can take things off your whitelist at any time, and delete things from your history. Eyebrowse also respects porn, ahem Private Browsing mode.
 more

Why?
  • For your friends! You look at lots of cool stuff on the web every day, but only share some of it. Eyebrowse is lightweight and non-invasive; you tell it once-per-site and it does the rest.
  • For you! How much time do you spend reading the news every day? Now you can get quantitative statistics and visualise long-term revisitation patterns. Eyebrowse personalizes your access to the web and recommends new content to you. More features are on the way!
  • For science! It's not fair that only the top search engine companies can do real web research these days. There should be public, OPEN corpora for use by researchers around the world.
  • It fosters awareness of what you do on the web: We are trying to raise awareness of what analytics companies are collecting about you as you surf the web, by showing you what you can learn from yourself.
  • It opens doors safely: By letting everyone selectively publish web trails in an open way we are hoping to foster a discussion of how we can use our web browsing behavior to build more adaptive and effective interfaces that RESPECT PEOPLE'S PRIVACY.

Questions? Write us at eyebrowse at csail dot mit dot edu.
